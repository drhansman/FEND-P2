Poopcat

A poopcat's greatest weakness is gravy.

  • Scientific Name: Felis cattus
  • Average Length: 45 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 14-16 years
  • Habitat: Sunny windowsills

Gray and brown and filled with speckles, the Poopcat spends the majority of its time sleeping, begging for food, and walking underfoot.
